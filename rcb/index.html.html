RCB's Middle Order Concerns After Close Loss to KKR

Royal Challengers Bangalore suffered a narrow 7-run defeat against Kolkata Knight Riders in a high-scoring thriller at Eden Gardens. Despite Virat Kohli's brilliant 72 and Glenn Maxwell's explosive 35, RCB fell short while chasing 198.

Coach Andy Flower expressed concerns about the middle-order batting, stating, "We need more contributions from numbers 4-7. While Virat and Maxi are in good form, we need the entire batting unit to fire consistently." The team is expected to make strategic changes before their next match against Delhi Capitals.
